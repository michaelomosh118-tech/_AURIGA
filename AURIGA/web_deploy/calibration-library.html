AURIGA // ACCESSIBILITY CALIBRATION

Pre-Calibrated Phone Library

Auriga's TruePath™ distance engine normally requires a one-time 10-point visual calibration using a physical 20×20cm colored square. For users who are blind or visually impaired — the people Auriga exists to serve — the visual square is a barrier, not a tool.

This library bypasses that step: pick your phone model from the list below and Auriga loads DrakoSanctis-certified preset measurements directly into the algorithm. All presets are sourced from manufacturer spec sheets (source: oem_spec) and human-population averages (source: derived) — never guessed.

Don't see your phone? Scroll to the CONTRIBUTE section. Submit your measurements with a short reference video; the DrakoSanctis team reviews and certifies every entry before it appears here. Unapproved contributions are never shown to the public.
